How to Record

  1. Click "Share Screen" and choose what to share
  2. Press "Start Recording" — the button turns red and pulses
  3. Perform your demo or presentation
  4. Click "Stop Recording" (same button) to download the video
  5. Use "Stop Sharing" when done
